    About Us

    Vistá specializes in crafting unforgettable hiking experiences, both local and overseas, for those who love exploring the outdoors. Whether you are looking for guided tours, flexible itineraries, or private group options, our certified mountain guides are here to help you enjoy the great outdoors. Join us for an adventure that will leave you with great memories and a deeper love for hiking!

    The Dolomites

    The Dolomites in northern Italy are part of the Alps and were listed as a World Natural and Cultural Heritage by UNESCO in 2009 for the unique and beautiful landscape. The 10-day tour combines the fun of trekking and wine-tasting, in which you will enjoy the rich, full-bodied flavors from local vineyards.

    Panorama Ginza Traverse

    The Panorama Ginza Traverse spans across multiple peaks of the northen Japanese Alps, including Mt. Tsubakuro, Mt. Otensho, Mt. Jonen and Mt. Chogatake. On this route you will encounter beautiful summer alpine flowers and, if lucky, Japanese rock ptarmigan, also known as thunder birds (raichō).

    Yushan

    Yushan, also known as Mount Jade, is the highest mountain in Taiwan with an elevation of 3,952m. The region is habitat to many endemic species, such as Formosan blue magpie, Formosan rock macaque and Formosan serow. In winter, you will get to see the magical views of snow-capped peaks.
